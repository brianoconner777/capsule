Our Vision

Our intent is to give every artist, excelling in all sorts of fields foreign to our rigid rationale, a chance to thrive under our mentorship and guidance and be an exemplar of following ones passion so that the ones who were hesitant or doubtful of our purpose and vision in the beginning, realize it and set their foot on our ever welcoming ship of HumanFOX.

Meet Our Editors

Capsule is like a container. A literal time capsule containing every detail of a persons life from his/her childhood to the last time they did something big in their life. The people we target for the interview, are the ones who made it big, which in reality is a relative term, so the ones who made it big enough for us to write about them so that their stories can inspire others. Its a beautiful ongoing process that keeps updating a persons admirers of their milestones achieved.
